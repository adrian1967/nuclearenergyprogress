Nuclear Energy Progress

Of all the energy sources available on the market—fossil fuels (coal, oil, and gas), hydroelectric power, and renewables (solar and wind power, biofuels)—nuclear energy is undoubtedly the most difficult to implement, both technically and in terms of public acceptance.

This website, created by an amateur, attempts to shed some light on the progress of this energy source, both technically and in terms of public acceptance.
