Why Nuclear Energy

Nuclear energy is one of the most powerful and reliable energy sources available today. It produces large amounts of electricity with very low carbon emissions.

Unlike fossil fuels, nuclear power does not rely on combustion and therefore does not release CO₂ during operation. This makes it a key technology in the fight against climate change.

Advanced reactor designs aim to improve safety, reduce waste and increase efficiency, making nuclear energy an even more viable long-term solution.
